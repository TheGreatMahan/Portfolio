Why Green Arrow?

The green Arrow is another hero that is trying to save his city by wearing mask and becoming a night vigilante. Oliver Queen was a millionair playboy who had no pressure on him until the age of 25. He then goes on a ship with his father. But their ship gets atacked by something. Almost everyone drowns except for him and his father. They were lost on the sea for a couple of days. His father sacrificed himself in orther to save Oliver. Oliver goes to Lian Yu island and learns martial arts and throwing arrows in there.

He faces many challenges during his stay in the Lian Yu island and learnes how to deal with any painful challenge. After a period of time he thinks that his city needs to be saved. As a result he goes back to Starling city.

Becoming the Green Arrow

After going back to the Starling City, He figures that the only way to serve his city is to become a vigilante who works seperately than the police of the city. He then wears a mask and a green suit and after people finding out about his presence called him the Green Arrow.
